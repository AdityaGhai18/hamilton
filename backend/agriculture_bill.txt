S. 2024
A BILL
To establish innovative water management solutions and agricultural resilience measures in response to rainfall shortages in the State of Delaware.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the "Delaware Water Resource Management and Agricultural Resilience Act of 2024".
SECTION 2. FINDINGS.
Congress finds that:
(1) The State of Delaware has experienced significant rainfall shortages, threatening agricultural productivity and economic stability;
(2) Traditional water management approaches have proven insufficient to address these challenges;
(3) Innovative solutions are required to ensure sustainable water resources for agricultural and public use.
